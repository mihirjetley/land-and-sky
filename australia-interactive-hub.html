Acknowledgment of Country. We acknowledge the Traditional Owners and Custodians of the lands, waters and skies across Australia, and pay our respects to Elders past and present. We also acknowledge Māori as tangata whenua of Aotearoa New Zealand. The seasonal and sky knowledge shown here has been shared publicly by First Nations Australian communities (through the Bureau of Meteorology’s Indigenous Weather Knowledge and CSIRO’s Indigenous seasonal calendars) and by Māori sources such as Te Papa and Te Ara. This knowledge belongs to its communities. Teachers are encouraged to connect with local Traditional Owners and iwi to learn the knowledge of their own area.
