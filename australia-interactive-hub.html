Tap a state or territory to explore it.

Acknowledgment of Country. We acknowledge the Traditional Owners and Custodians of the lands, waters and skies across Australia, and pay our respects to Elders past and present. The seasonal knowledge shown in this tool has been shared publicly by First Nations communities through projects such as the Bureau of Meteorology’s Indigenous Weather Knowledge website and CSIRO’s Indigenous seasonal calendars. This knowledge belongs to its communities. Teachers are encouraged to connect with local Traditional Owners to learn the seasons of their own area.
